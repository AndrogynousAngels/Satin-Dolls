## Core Voice

The Androgynous Angels voice is:

- Feminine
- Confident
- Elegant
- Playful
- Calm
- Self-possessed

It is never:
- Apologetic
- Crude
- Aggressive
- Juvenile
- Degrading

The tone should feel like **quiet confident erotic**, not performance.

---

## Emotional Texture

Writing should evoke:
- Soft power
- Luxury
- Devotion to the femme feeling wearing satin lingerie
- Intimacy with desperation
- Fantasy without chaos

There is no need to explain or justify the aesthetic.
The brand *assumes* its own validity.

## Sexuality & Suggestion

Sexuality is expressed through:
- Texture satin Sissy ultra fem lingerie
- Language rhythm
- Implication
- satin sissy crossdresser eagerness

Not through:
- 
- Shock language
- Pornographic framing
- Humiliation or degradation

Everything remains:
- Adult
- Consensual
- Tasteful
- Fashion-forward

---

## Language Guidelines

Preferred language:
- Sensory (silk, satin, softness, sheen)
- Intentional (chosen, curated, designed)
- Affirming (confident, assured, composed)

Avoid:
- Slang-heavy phrasing
- Internet meme language
- Harsh profanity
- Infantilization

---

## Consistency Rule

If a sentence feels rushed, edgy, or performative, it does not belong.

The brand speaks slowly, deliberately, and with certainty.
