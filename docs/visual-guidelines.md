# Androgynous Angels — Visual Guidelines

This document defines the visual identity and aesthetic rules for the
Androgynous Angels brand and its primary storefront:

https://www.stepforddolls.com

These guidelines apply to:
- Website imagery
- AI-generated images
- Social media visuals
- Product presentation
- Promotional graphics

---

## Core Aesthetic
- Sissy, Crossdresser, Transgender
- Ultra-feminine, satin fetish
- Satin-forward (silk, satin, nylon emphasis)
- Vintage-inspired (1940s–1960s Stepford, boudoir, pin-up)
- Elegant, playful, and polished
- Fashion-editorial 

The visual tone should feel:
- Luxurious
- Intentional
- Soft, teasing, satin fetish
- Fantastical, Vintage, Ultra Fem, 

---

## Color & Texture

- Primary materials: satin, silk, nylon
- Soft lighting preferred
- High-gloss fabric highlights
- Pastels, creams, whites, pinks, reds, blacks (context-dependent)

Avoid:
- Harsh shadows
- Gritty or industrial textures
- Modern streetwear aesthetics

---

## Models & Presentation

- Two adult models only both blonde
- Coordinated styling
- Balanced composition
- Clear focus on garments and materials
- Tasteful, erotic posing

---

## Consistency Rule

All visuals should feel like they belong to the same universe.

If an image feels out of place, it does not get used.
